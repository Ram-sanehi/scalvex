

# Edge Services Agency Website - Implementation Plan

## Overview
A modern, professional 6-page agency website for Edge Services (edgeservices.in) with a focus on trust, clarity, and conversion. Remote-first positioning targeting US/international clients.

---

## Design Foundation

**Color System**
- Primary: Deep Blue (#0F2A44) - headers, buttons, accents
- Text: Charcoal Gray (#1F2933) - body text, secondary elements  
- Background: Soft White (#F9FAFB) - page backgrounds
- Accent: Muted Sky Blue (#3BAFDA) - minimal highlights, hover states

**Typography & Layout**
- Inter font family throughout
- Max content width: 1200px with generous whitespace
- Section padding: 80-120px for breathing room
- Subtle rounded corners and shadows on cards
- Gentle fade-in animations on scroll

---
